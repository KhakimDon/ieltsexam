Writing Test Instructions

General Instructions

  • Total time: 60 minutes for both tasks
  • Task 1: Spend about 20 minutes, write at least 150 words
  • Task 2: Spend about 40 minutes, write at least 250 words
  • You can switch between tasks at any time
  • Your work is auto-saved every 5 seconds

Task 1 - Academic Writing

  • Describe visual information (graph, chart, diagram, map)
  • Summarize main features and make comparisons
  • Use formal, objective language
  • Include an overview paragraph
  • Support descriptions with specific data

Task 2 - Essay Writing

  • Present a clear position on the topic
  • Develop your argument with reasons and examples
  • Organize ideas into clear paragraphs
  • Use a range of vocabulary and grammar structures
  • Include introduction, body paragraphs, and conclusion

Assessment Criteria

  • Task Achievement: Answer all parts of the question
  • Coherence & Cohesion: Organize ideas logically
  • Lexical Resource: Use varied vocabulary accurately
  • Grammatical Range: Use complex structures correctly

Tips for Success

  • Plan your answer before writing (3-5 minutes)
  • Leave time to check your work (3-5 minutes)
  • Count words regularly to meet requirements
  • Stay on topic and answer the question directly
  • Use paragraphs to organize your ideas clearly
